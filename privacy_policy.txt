Privacy Policy for Palette Peek

Palette Peek is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection and Use:
Palette Peek does not collect or store any personal information. The extension only accesses the active tab to extract color and font information, which is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit any data to external servers.

Data Storage:
Any palettes you choose to save are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
